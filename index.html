About us

Kombucha is a fermented tea that has become increasingly popular in recent years due to its health benefits and unique flavor.

Our company is dedicated to producing the highest quality kombucha using only the finest ingredients and traditional brewing methods.

We offer a variety of flavors, from classic to more unique combinations, to cater to all tastes. Our kombucha is available in bottles or on tap at select retailers and restaurants.

Whether you're a kombucha enthusiast or a first-time drinker, our company is committed to providing you with a delicious and healthy beverage that you can feel good about consuming.

We start with organic tea leaves and pure filtered water, which are combined and brewed to create a flavorful base. To this, we add a symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeast, also known as a SCOBY, which initiates the fermentation process.
